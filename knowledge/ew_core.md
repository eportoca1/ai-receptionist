# Electronic World — Core Company + Policies (Internal Use)

## Company snapshot
- Electronic World (EW) designs and manufactures mobile accessories.
- Product categories include: screen protection, cases/device protection, charging & power, audio, mounts, and lifestyle accessories.
- Texas-based operations and fulfillment.

## Wholesale / dealer basics (internal script)
- No minimum order quantities.
- Dealers can order through a wholesale online portal (once approved).
- Wholesale orders over $250 ship free.
- Each wholesale account receives a dedicated account manager for support.

## Shipping (consumer/shop)
- Fulfillment ships from Sugar Land, Texas.
- Processing time: typically 1–2 business days (Mon–Fri, excluding holidays).
- UPS Ground: estimated 2–3 business days transit after processing.
- UPS Expedited: estimated 1–2 business days transit after processing.

## Returns & Warranty (consumer/shop)
- Returns are primarily within 14 days (policy-specific rules apply).
- 1-year limited warranty for manufacturer defects (separate from return window).
- Warranty generally requires proof of purchase.
- Warranty excludes physical damage, liquid exposure, and normal wear.
